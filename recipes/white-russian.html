White Russian

A classic White Russian cocktail made with coffee liqueur, vodka, and either cream or milk.

Ingredients

  • Â½ cup cream or milk
  • 1 fluid ounce coffee flavored liqueur
  • 1 fluid ounce vodka
  • 1 cup ice

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Combine cream, coffee-flavored liqueur, and vodka in a glass.
  3. Pour over ice in a highball glass.
